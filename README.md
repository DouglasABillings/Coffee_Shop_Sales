# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard — Excel Analytics

## Overview
Built an interactive Excel dashboard for **Maven Roasters**, a 3-location NYC coffee chain, using 6 months of transactional data (Jan–Jun 2023). The dashboard gives franchise owners a single view of revenue trends, traffic patterns, and product performance — designed for decisions, not just display.

**Skills demonstrated:** Data preparation · PivotTables · Dashboard design · Business insight communication  
**Tools:** Microsoft Excel

---

## What I Built

### Data Preparation
- Cleaned and structured raw transactional records for PivotTable compatibility
- Derived time-based fields (hour of day, day of week, month) to enable traffic pattern analysis
- Validated category and product-type consistency across 140,000+ transactions

### Dashboard
An interactive Excel dashboard with slicers for location filtering, covering:

- **Monthly revenue trend** — steady growth from $81K (Jan) to $166K (Jun)
- **Hourly traffic** — peak volume at 8–10am, sharp drop after noon
- **Daily traffic** — relatively even Mon–Fri, with a notable Saturday dip
- **Category breakdown** — Coffee and Tea dominate; Packaged Chocolate and Loose Tea trail significantly
- **Product-level revenue** — Brewed Chai Tea ($77K) and Barista Espresso ($91K) are top earners

---

## Key Findings

| Finding | Business Implication |
|---|---|
| 70%+ of transactions occur before 11am | Staffing and prep should front-load the morning shift |
| Coffee Beans, Loose Tea, and Packaged Chocolate underperform | Bundle with beverage purchases or reduce shelf space |
| Saturday traffic is lowest day of the week | Opportunity for weekend promotions or adjusted hours |
| Revenue grew 104% from January to June | Strong upward trajectory — worth investigating what drove March–April acceleration |
